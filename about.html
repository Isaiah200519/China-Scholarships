About China Scholarships Hub

Connecting students with educational opportunities in China since 2018

Our Story

China Scholarships Hub was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to make quality education in China accessible to students from around the world. Our founders, former international students in China themselves, recognized the challenges students faced in finding legitimate scholarships and navigating the complex application process.

What started as a small initiative to help a few friends and family members has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting thousands of students with Chinese universities. Today, we partner with over 150 Chinese universities and maintain a network of 50+ verified agents across Africa and other regions.

We believe that education is the most powerful tool for personal and societal transformation. By bridging the gap between talented students and world-class educational opportunities in China, we're helping to build a generation of globally-minded leaders.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality higher education in China by providing transparent, comprehensive scholarship information and personalized guidance to students worldwide. We are committed to breaking down barriers and creating equal opportunities for all talented individuals to pursue their academic dreams in China.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for international education in China, recognized for our integrity, innovation, and impact. We envision a world where every qualified student, regardless of background, can access world-class education and contribute to global understanding and development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform

How do you verify scholarships and agents?

We have a rigorous verification process. For scholarships, we work directly with university admissions offices to confirm details. For agents, we conduct background checks, verify credentials, and require proof of successful student placements. All agents sign our code of ethics agreement.

Is your service completely free for students?

Yes, our platform is completely free for students. We never charge students for accessing scholarship information or using our application portal. We earn revenue through university partnerships and agent commissions, ensuring students can access our services at no cost.

How long does the application process take?

The application timeline varies by university and program. Generally, it takes 2-4 weeks to prepare a strong application, plus 4-8 weeks for university processing. We recommend starting 6-8 months before your intended start date to allow time for document preparation, language tests, and visa processing.

Do I need to know Chinese to study in China?

Not necessarily. Many Chinese universities offer programs taught entirely in English, especially at the graduate level. However, learning basic Chinese is recommended for daily life. Some scholarships even include a 1-year Chinese language preparatory course for students in Chinese-taught programs.

Can I work while studying in China?

International students in China can work part-time (up to 20 hours per week) with permission from their university and the local authorities. However, this is usually limited to on-campus jobs or specific internships. Most scholarships provide a living stipend that covers basic expenses.
