Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Check Spam Folder: Reset emails sometimes go to spam. Check your junk/spam folder first.
  2. Wait 2-3 minutes: Firebase email delivery can take a minute or two. Refresh after waiting.
  3. Verify Your Email: Make sure you entered the correct email address.
  4. Use Different Email: Try with a Gmail or Outlook account to test.
  5. Check Firebase Console:
    • Go to Firebase Console → Authentication → Templates
    • Check if "Password Reset" template is enabled
    • Verify sender email is set to noreply@[your-project].firebaseapp.com
  6. Check Email Domain Restrictions: Some corporate email systems block Firebase emails. Use a personal email to test.
  7. Clear Cache: Clear browser cache and cookies, then try again.
